Analysis and Design of “Eatzy” Application on Android Based Mobile Devices Using the RUP Method

Eatzy
  • Project: Publication ARS 2019

This project was made during my third year in Sriwijaya University and presented on Proceeding Annual Research Seminar (ARS) 2019 - Faculty Of Computer Science, Sriwijaya University. We used Rational Unified Process (RUP) approach in designing an android mobile-base application called "Eatzy" to help users find recommendation for food, beverage recipes and restaurant recommendations.
